About Our Company

History

Founded in 1995, TeaTime E-commerce began as a small store in Melbourne. With passion and commitment to quality, we quickly grew in reputation and size. Over the years, we've expanded our offerings and now proudly serve customers worldwide.

Ethos

Our ethos is rooted in three principles:

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Sustainability: Every step in our supply chain considers the well-being of our planet.
  • Community: We believe in giving back and growing together as a community.

Milestones

Over the years, we've achieved significant milestones:

  • Opened our 100th store in 2010.
  • Launched our online platform in 2015, bringing our products to global customers.
  • Introduced eco-friendly packaging in 2020, reinforcing our commitment to sustainability.
