Cook Position

Job Overview

We're on the lookout for a passionate and dedicated Cook to join our culinary team in Melbourne, VIC. As a Cook, you'll play a vital role in crafting delicious cookies and treats for our valued customers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Preparing and cooking items according to recipes.
  • Maintaining a sanitized and orderly kitchen environment.
  • Ensuring food quality and freshness.
  • Monitoring stock and placing orders as needed.
  • Working closely with the culinary team to ensure customer satisfaction.

Qualifications

  • Proven experience as a Cook.
  • Familiarity with kitchen equipment and utensils.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Knowledge of health and safety regulations.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary.
  • Flexible work hours.
  • Health benefits.
  • Opportunity for growth and advancement.
