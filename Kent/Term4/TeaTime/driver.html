Driver Position

Job Overview

We're searching for a dedicated and responsible Driver to ensure timely delivery of our quality products to our customers. The role is based in Sydney, NSW.

Key Responsibilities

  • Delivering orders to customers on specific routes.
  • Reviewing orders before and after delivery to ensure orders are complete.
  • Maintaining an organized travel schedule.
  • Ensuring that vehicles have sufficient fuel and are always ready for use.
  • Documenting and logging work/rest periods and kilometers spent driving.

Qualifications

  • Valid driver's license.
  • Clean driving record.
  • Proven experience as a Driver.
  • Knowledge of area roads and neighborhoods.
  • Effective communication skills.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary.
  • Health benefits.
  • Retirement plans.
  • On-the-job training.
