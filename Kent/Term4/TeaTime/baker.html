Baker Position

Job Overview

TeaTime E-commerce is in search of a skilled and passionate Baker to join our team in Brisbane, QLD. As our Baker, you will be at the heart of our operations, creating delightful baked goods that keep our customers coming back for more.

Key Responsibilities

  • Preparing traditional and creative baked goods following original recipes.
  • Operating baking equipment, like ovens, mixers, and dough sheeters.
  • Maintaining and cleaning baking tools, utensils, and equipment.
  • Decorating cakes, cookies, and other pastries as required.
  • Ensuring all baked items are completed on time for opening or specific events.

Qualifications

  • Proven experience as a Baker.
  • Familiarity with baking techniques and the professional kitchen equipment.
  • Attention to detail.
  • Ability to work in high-pressure situations.
  • Knowledge of food safety regulations.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and bonus opportunities.
  • Training and development programs.
  • Health and dental benefits.
  • Discounts on our products.
